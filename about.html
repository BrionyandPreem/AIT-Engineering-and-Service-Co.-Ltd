    History & Expertise

    Established in September 2002

    AIT Engineering and Service Co., Ltd. aims to be Thailand’s leading instrument and service provider for Field Instruments, Analyzers, Transmitters and Actuation Technologies in various industries.

    Providing engineering and services to meet growing customer demands.

    Global Portfolio

    BETTIS, EIM, EL O MATIC, SHAFER, and HYTORK (Actuation), ROSEMOUNT (Field Instruments), MICROMOTION (Flow) and CAMILLE BAUER (Isolators and Angle transmitters).

    Water resource management impacts a lot in industrial processes. We partnered with waste water treatment solution experts in expanding our services and offerings for DAP floats and trenners.

    We offer the best solution to our customers with our well trained staffs and successfully proven products. We have the right people with the right products for demanding processes. We are confident to provide our expertise to our customers.

    Milestones

    2002 – AIT Engineering and Services was established.

    2005 – Added office in Banpong Area near SCG Packaging

    2006 – Appointed by Emerson Process Management as local partner

    2010 – Added office in Khon Kaen (Northeast Thailand)

    2012 – Opened Rayong office for valves and actuators

    2013 – ISO Standards implementation for the whole company.

    2014 – Applied for Bureau Veritas Seal

    2015

    • Passed the Bureau Veritas Certification and was certified for Sales, installation and after sales of measuring devices and Automation systems.
    • Relocated the Rayong office to its current place with the new 500 sq.m workshop and 5 ton overhead crane for the service team.
    • Appointed local business partner of UMV

    2018 – Signed contract with PMT S.r.l

    2020 – Signed contract with Airaghi S.r.L. (refiner plate), Weidmuller (DCS & power supply)
