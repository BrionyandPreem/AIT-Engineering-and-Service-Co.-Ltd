    History

    Established in September 2002,

    AIT Engineering and Service Co., Ltd. aims to be Thailand's leading instrument and service provider for Field Instruments, Analyzers, Transmitters and Actuation Technologies in various industries.

    Providing engineering and services to meet growing customer demands.

    Our portfolio consists of BETTIS, EIM, EL O MATIC, SHAFER, and HYTORK Actuation), ROSEMOUNT (Field Instruments), MICROMOTION (Flow) and CAMILLE BAUER (Isolators and Angle transmitters).

    We offer the best solution to our customers with our well trained staffs and successfully proven products.

    • 2002 – AIT Engineering and Services was established.
    • 2005 – Added office in Banpong Area near SCG Packaging.

    2006 – Appointed by Emerson Process Management as the local business partner for the Pulp and paper industry.

    2010 – Added another office in Khon Kaen (North east Thailand)

    2012 – Rented an office in Rayong and started selling valves and actuators in that area.

    2013 – Started to use ISO Standards for the whole company operations.

    2014 – Applied for the Bureau Veritas Seal for management system standards.

    2015 – Passed the Bureau Veritas Certification and was certified for Sales, installation and after sales.

    • Relocated the Rayong office to its current place with the new 500 sq.m workshop.

    2018 – Signed contract with PMT S.r.l

    2020 – Signed contract with Airaghi S.r.l.
